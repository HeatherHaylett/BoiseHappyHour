# Privacy Policy

**App:** B4Five
**Effective date:** May 31, 2026
**Contact:** haylett.development@gmail.com

## What information we collect

B4Five does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app does not require an account, login, or any identifying information to use.

## How the app works

All venue data is bundled directly into the app at build time. The app makes no network requests at runtime and functions fully offline. No data is sent to any server.

## Device permissions

B4Five does not request any device permissions. The app does not access your location, camera, contacts, microphone, or any other device data.

## Third-party services

B4Five does not use any third-party analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

## Children's privacy

B4Five is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to this policy

If the app is updated in a future version to collect any user data, this policy will be updated and the effective date will change. No data collection will be added without updating this document.
